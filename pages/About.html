ABOUT

The 𝐓𝐨𝐮𝐫𝐢𝐬𝐦 𝐒𝐭𝐮𝐝𝐞𝐧𝐭 𝐎𝐫𝐠𝐚𝐧𝐢𝐳𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 logo is a testament to students trailblazing a path to excellence in the field of tourism while bringing with them competencies in the four vital components of tourism (attraction, accommodation, accessibility, and amenities). The concept and design were analyzed to represent the different symbols that our industry has. The color palette of the logo was based on the color of the Tourism Industry in the Philippines; Orange is the color of food tourism, blue for dive tourism, violet for culture and heritage, yellow represents faith tourism, and pink to inspire the students to remain optimistic in their path and have a positive and radiant manner.
